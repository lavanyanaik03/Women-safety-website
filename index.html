Self-Defense Tips

1. Stay aware of your surroundings Avoid walking with full-volume earphones, notice exits, lighting, and people around you.
2. Trust your instincts If something feels unsafe, leave immediately without hesitation.
3. Keep emergency tools ready Keep your phone charged and a whistle/self-defense tool if possible.
4. Use your voice Shout loudly for help—your voice is a powerful tool.
5. Basic physical defense Target sensitive areas like eyes, nose, throat, groin, knees.
